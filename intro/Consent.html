You are being invited to take part in a study of how people form connections and communicate in a small online network over two sessions, about a week apart. During the live session you will exchange short messages with other participants. Some participants may use, and you may encounter, AI-assisted messages during the study.

Participation is voluntary. You may withdraw at any time without penalty. Your responses will be de-identified before analysis. Session recordings (message logs, survey responses) are retained only for research purposes.

If you have questions about this study, contact the research team using the information provided on the recruitment page.
